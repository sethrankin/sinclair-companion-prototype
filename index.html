The daily loop

  • The question is "are you drinking today", not "did you stay dry". That is what makes it TSM.
  • Say yes and it prompts the tablet and runs the one-hour timer before you log any drinks.
  • Drinks are one tap each, auto-converted to units. One craving rating closes the day.
  • Progress shows the extinction curve and your tablet-before-drinking rate, the number that predicts success.
  • Say no and there is nothing to do; no tablet on dry days.
